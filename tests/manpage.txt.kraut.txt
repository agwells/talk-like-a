.SH "DESCR-r-rIPTION"
All uff dese programs are filters to do all sorts uff strange dingkts to text.
Nein personal, r-r-racial, r-r-religious or societal slurs are intended. For amusement
only.
.P
All ze filters r-r-read input from stdin, khange it, undt w-r-rite ze filtered
text to stdout. Some filters also support r-r-readingkt from files and
w-r-ritingkt to stdout.
.IP b1ff
Ze B1FF filter
.IP lolcat
As seen in internet gifs eferywhere.
.IP kockney
Cockney Englisch
.IP khef
confert Englisch on stdin to Mock Swedisch on stdout
.IP eleet
K3wl hacker slang
.IP fanboy
Speak like a fanboy. Filters out extraneous vords undt focuses on ze words
fans use. By default, it vill speak like a fan uff git/Linus/linux
defelopment. To khange dis, pass as parameters ze words dat ze fanboy
typically uses. Alternatifely, pass ze name uff a topic dat typically has
fanboys to use a predefined vord list.
.IP fudd
Elmer Fudd
.IP jedro
Hillbilly text filter
.IP jibberisch
R-r-runs text drough a r-r-random selection uff de r-r-rest uff de filters, to make r-r-really
weird output.
.IP ken
Englisch into Kockney, featuringkt (dubious) r-r-rhymingkt
slang for a lot uff computer terminology.
.IP kraut
Generates text mitt a bad German accent.
.IP kenny
Generates text as spoken by Kenny on Soud Park.
.IP ky00te
Dis program places a fery kute (and familiar to FurryMuck
fans) accent on any text file.
.IP nedackify
Viped out text like kan be found in nedack.
.IP newspeak
A-la-1984
.IP kensor
CDA-ize text
.IP nyc
Brooklyn Englisch
.IP pirate
Talk like a pirate.
.IP r-r-rasterman
Makes text look like it kame from ze keyboard uff Carsten Haitzler.
.IP scottisch
Fake scottisch (dwarfen) accent filter, inspired by ze character "Durkon"
from Order uff de Stick.
.IP spammer
Turns honest text into somedingkt dat ist liable to be flagged as spam.
.IP scramble
Scramble ze "inner" letters uff each vord in ze input into a r-r-random order.
Ze resultingkt text ist still strangely r-r-readable.
.IP studly
Studly kaps.
.IP uniencode
Use glorious unicode to ze fullest possible extent. As seen prefiously in
many man pages.
.IP upside\-down
Flips text upside down. Stand on your head undt squint to r-r-read ze output.
