.SH "DESCRIPTION"
All of vese programs are filters to do all sorts of strange fings to text.
No personal, racial, religious or societal slurs are intended. For amusement
only.
.P
All the bloody filters read input from stdin, change i', 'n' write the filtered
text to stdout. Some filters also support readin' from files 'n'
writin' to stdout.
.IP b1ff
The flinkin' B1FF filter
.IP lolcat
As seen in internet gifs everywhere.
.IP cockney
Cockney English
.IP chef
convert English on stdin to Mock Swedish on stdout
.IP eleet
K3wl 'acker slang
.IP fanboy
Speak like a fanboy. Filters out extraneous words 'n' focuses on the words
fans use. By default, i' will speak like a fan of git/Linus/linux
development. To change this, pass as parameters the bleedin' words that the fanboy
typically uses. Alternatively, pass the soddin' nime of a topic that typically 'as
fanboys to use a predefined word list.
.IP fudd
Elmer Fudd
.IP jefro
'illbilly text filter
.IP jibberish
Runs text frough a random selection of the rest of the bloody filters, to mike really
weird output.
.IP ken
English into Cockney, featurin' (dubious) rhymin'
slang for a lot of computer terminology.
.IP kraut
Generates text wif a bad German accent.
.IP kenny
Generates text as spoken by Kenny on Sowf Park.
.IP ky00te
This program places a very cute ('n' familiar to FurryMuck
fans) accent on enny text file.
.IP nefackify
Wiped out text like can be found in nefack.
.IP newspeak
A-la-1984
.IP censor
CDA-oize text
.IP nyc
Brooklyn English
.IP pirate
Talk like a pirate.
.IP rasterman
Makes text look like i' cime from the keyboard of Carsten 'aitzler.
.IP scottish
Fike scottish (dwarven) accent filter, inspired by the flinkin' character "Durkon"
from Order of the Stick.
.IP spammer
Turns 'onest text into somefin' that is liable to be flagged as spam.
.IP scramble
Scramble the bleedin' "inner" letters of each word in the input into a random order.
The soddin' resultin' text is still strangely readable.
.IP studly
Studly caps.
.IP uniencode
Use glorious unicode to the fullest possible extent. As seen previously in
menny man pages.
.IP upside\-down
Flips text upside down. Stand on yer 'ead 'n' squint to read the bloody output.
