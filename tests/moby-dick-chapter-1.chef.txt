Cell me-a Ishmeel. Sume-a yeers egu — nefer meend hoo lung preceesely — hefeeng leettle-a oor nu muney in my poorse-a, und nutheeng perteecooler tu interest me-a oon shure-a, I thuooght I vuoold seeel ebuoot a leettle-a und see-a zee vetery pert ooff zee vurld. It is a vey I hefe-a ooff dreefing oofff zee spleee und regooleteeng zee ceercooleshun. Vhunefer I feend myselff grooeeng greem ebuoot zee muoot; vhunefer it is a demp, dreezzly Nufember in my suool; vhunefer I feend myselff infuloontereely pooseeng beffure-a cufffeen verehuooses, und breenging up zee reer ooff ifery foonerel I meet; und ispeceeelly vhunefer my hypus get sooch un upper hund ooff me-a, thet it reqooeeres a strung murel preenciple-a tu prefunt me-a frum deleeberetely steppeeng intu zee street, und methudeecelly knuckeeng peuple-a’s hets oofff — zeee, I eccuoont it heegh teeme-a tu get tu sea es suun es I cun. Thees is my soobsteetoote-a fur peestul und bell. Veet a pheelusuphicel fluooreesh Cetu throos heemselff upun hees svurd; I qooeeetly teke-a tu zee sheep. Zeere-a is nutheeng soorpreesing in thees. Iff zeey boot knev it, elmust ell mee in zeeeer degree-a, sume-a teeme-a oor oozeer, chereesh fery neerly zee seme-a feeleengs tooerds zee ooceun veet me-a.

Zeere-a noo is yuoor insooler ceety ooff zee Munhettues, belted ruoond by vherfes es Indeeun isles by curel reeffs — cummerce-a soorruoonds it veet her soorff. Reeght und lefft, zee streets teke-a yuoo veterverd. Its ixtreme-a doontoon is zee bettery, vhere-a thet nuble-a mule-a is veshed by vefes, und cuuled by breezes, vheech a fev huoors prefeeuoos vere-a oooot ooff seeght ooff lund. Luuk et zee croods ooff veter-gezers zeere-a.
Bork Bork Bork!

Ceercoomemboolete-a zee ceety ooff a dreemy Sebbet effternuun. Gu frum Curleers Huuk tu Cuunteees Sleep, und frum zeunce-a, by Vheetehell, nurthverd. Vhet du yuoo see-a? — Pusted leeke-a seelunt sunteenels ell eruoond zee toon, stund thuoosunds upun thuoosunds ooff murtel mee feexed in ooceun refereees. Sume-a leuneeng egeeenst zee speeles; sume-a seeted upun zee peeer-heeds; sume-a luukeeng oofer zee boolverks ooff sheeps frum Cheena; sume-a heegh elufft in zee reegging, es iff streefing tu get a steell better seeverd peep. Boot zeese-a ere-a ell lundsmee; ooff veek deys punt up in let und plester — teeed tu cuoonters, neeeled tu bunches, cleenched tu desks. Hoo zeee is thees? Ere-a zee greee feeelds gune-a? Vhet du zeey here-a?
Bork Bork Bork!

Boot luuk! here-a cume-a mure-a croods, peceeng streeeght fur zee veter, und seemeengly buoond fur a deefe-a. Strunge-a! Nutheeng veell cuntunt zeem boot zee ixtremest leemit ooff zee lund; lueetering under zee shedy lee-a ooff yunder verehuooses veell nut soofffeece-a. Nu. Zeey moost get joost es neegh zee veter es zeey pusseebly cun veethuoot felleeng in. Und zeere-a zeey stund — meeles ooff zeem — leegooes. Inlunders ell, zeey cume-a frum lunes und elleys, streets und efunooes — nurt, iest, suoot, und vest. Yet here-a zeey ell uneete-a. Tell me-a, dues zee megneteec feertooe-a ooff zee needles ooff zee cumpesses ooff ell thuse-a sheeps ettrect zeem theezeer?
Bork Bork Bork!

Oonce-a mure-a. Sey yuoo ere-a in zee cuoontry; in sume-a heegh lund ooff lekes. Teke-a elmust uny pet yuoo pleese-a, und tee tu oone-a it cerreees yuoo doon in a dele-a, und leefes yuoo zeere-a by a puul in zee streem. Zeere-a is megeec in it. Let zee must ebsunt-meended ooff mee be-a ploonged in hees deepest refereees — stund thet mun oon hees legs, set hees feet a-gueeng, und he-a veell inffelleebly leed yuoo tu veter, iff veter zeere-a be-a in ell thet regeeun. Shuoold yuoo ifer be-a etheerst in zee greet Emereecun desert, try thees ixpereemunt, iff yuoor cerefun heppee tu be-a sooppleeed veet a metephyseecel pruffessur. Yes, es ifery oone-a knoos, medeeteshun und veter ere-a vedded fur ifer.
Bork Bork Bork!

Boot here-a is un erteest. He-a deseeres tu peeent yuoo zee dreemeeest, shedeeest, qooeeetest, must inchunteeng beet ooff rumunteec lundscepe-a in ell zee felley ooff zee Secu. Vhet is zee cheeeff ilemunt he-a impluys? Zeere-a stund hees trees, iech veet a hulloo troonk, es iff a hermeet und a crooceeffix vere-a veethin; und here-a sleeps hees meedoo, und zeere-a sleep hees cettle-a; und up frum yunder cuttege-a gues a sleepy smuke-a. Deep intu deestunt vuudlunds veends a mezy vey, reecheeng tu ooferleppeeng spoors ooff muoonteeens bezeed in zeeeer heell-seede-a blooe-a. Boot thuoogh zee peectoore-a leees thoos trunced, und thuoogh thees peene-a-tree-a shekes doon its seeghs leeke-a leefes upun thees shepherd’s heed, yet ell vere-a feeen, unless zee shepherd’s iye-a vere-a feexed upun zee megeec streem beffure-a heem. Gu feesit zee Preeeries in Joone-a, vhee fur scures oon scures ooff meeles yuoo vede-a knee-a-deep emung Teeger-leelies — vhet is zee oone-a cherm vunteeng? — Veter — zeere-a is nut a drup ooff veter zeere-a! Vere-a Neeegera boot a ceterect ooff sund, vuoold yuoo trefel yuoor thuoosund meeles tu see-a it? Vhy deed zee puur puet ooff Tunnessee-a, upun sooddunly receeefing tvu hundffools ooff seelfer, deleeberete-a vhezeer tu booy heem a cuet, vheech he-a sedly needed, oor infest hees muney in a pedestreeun treep tu Ruckevey Beech? Vhy is elmust ifery ruboost heelthy buy veet a ruboost heelthy suool in heem, et sume-a teeme-a oor oozeer crezy tu gu tu sea? Vhy upun yuoor feerst fuyege-a es a pessunger, deed yuoo yuoorselff feel sooch a mysteecel feebreshun, vhee feerst tuld thet yuoo und yuoor sheep vere-a noo oooot ooff seeght ooff lund? Vhy deed zee oold Perseeuns huld zee sea huly? Vhy deed zee Greeks geefe-a it a seperete-a deeety, und oovn bruzeer ooff Jufe-a? Soorely ell thees is nut veethuoot meuneeng. Und steell deeper zee meuneeng ooff thet stury ooff Nerceessoos, vhu becoose-a he-a cuoold nut gresp zee turmunteeng, meeld imege-a he-a sev in zee fuoonteeen, ploonged intu it und ves drooned. Boot thet seme-a imege-a, ve-a oooorselfes see-a in ell reefers und ooceuns. It is zee imege-a ooff zee ungrespeble-a phuntum ooff leeffe-a; und thees is zee key tu it ell.
Bork Bork Bork!

Noo, vhee I sey thet I em in zee hebeet ooff gueeng tu sea vhunefer I begeen tu groo hezy ebuoot zee iyes, und begeen tu be-a oofer cunsceeuoos ooff my loongs, I du nut meun tu hefe-a it infferred thet I ifer gu tu sea es a pessunger. Fur tu gu es a pessunger yuoo moost needs hefe-a a poorse-a, und a poorse-a is boot a reg unless yuoo hefe-a sumetheeng in it. Beseedes, pessungers get sea-seeck — groo qooerrelsume-a — dun’t sleep ooff neeghts — du nut injuy zeemselfes mooch, es a gunerel theeng; — nu, I nefer gu es a pessunger; nur, thuoogh I em sumetheeng ooff a selt, du I ifer gu tu sea es a Cummudure-a, oor a Cepteeen, oor a Cuuk. I ebundun zee glury und deestincshun ooff sooch oofffeeces tu thuse-a vhu leeke-a zeem. Fur my pert, I ebumeenete-a ell hunureble-a respecteble-a tueels, treeels, und treebooleshuns ooff ifery keend vhetsuefer. It is qooeete-a es mooch es I cun du tu teke-a cere-a ooff myselff, veethuoot tekeeng cere-a ooff sheeps, berqooes, breegs, schuuners, und vhet nut. Und es fur gueeng es cuuk, — thuoogh I cunffess zeere-a is cunseedereble-a glury in thet, a cuuk beeeng a surt ooff oofffeecer oon sheep-buerd — yet, sumehoo, I nefer funceeed brueeling fools; — thuoogh oonce-a brueeled, joodeeciuoosly boottered, und joodgmeteecelly selted und peppered, zeere-a is nu oone-a vhu veell speek mure-a respectffoolly, nut tu sey referunteeelly, ooff a brueeled fool thun I veell. It is oooot ooff zee iduletruoos duteengs ooff zee oold Igypteeuns upun brueeled ibees und ruested reefer hurse-a, thet yuoo see-a zee moommeees ooff thuse-a creetoores in zeeeer hooge-a beke-a-huooses zee pyremeeds.
Bork Bork Bork!

Nu, vhee I gu tu sea, I gu es a seemple-a seeelur, reeght beffure-a zee mest, ploomb doon intu zee furecestle-a, elufft zeere-a tu zee ruyel mest-heed. Trooe-a, zeey rezeer oorder me-a ebuoot sume-a, und meke-a me-a joomp frum sper tu sper, leeke-a a gresshupper in a Mey meedoo. Und et feerst, thees surt ooff theeng is unpleesunt inuoogh. It tuooches oone-a’s sunse-a ooff hunur, perteecoolerly iff yuoo cume-a ooff un oold istebleeshed femeely in zee lund, zee Fun Runsseleers, oor Rundulphs, oor Herdeecunootes. Und mure-a thun ell, iff joost prefeeuoos tu pootteeng yuoor hund intu zee ter-put, yuoo hefe-a beee lurdeeng it es a cuoontry schuulmester, mekeeng zee tellest buys stund in eve-a ooff yuoo. Zee trunseeshun is a keee oone-a, I essoore-a yuoo, frum a schuulmester tu a seeelur, und reqooeeres a strung decucshun ooff Suneca und zee Stueecs tu ineble-a yuoo tu green und beer it. Boot ifee thees veers oofff in teeme-a.
Bork Bork Bork!

Vhet ooff it, iff sume-a oold hoonks ooff a sea-cepteeen oorders me-a tu get a bruum und sveep doon zee decks? Vhet dues thet indeegnity emuoont tu, veeeghed, I meun, in zee sceles ooff zee Nev Testemunt? Du yuoo theenk zee erchungel Gebreeel theenks unytheeng zee less ooff me-a, becoose-a I prumptly und respectffoolly oobey thet oold hoonks in thet perteecooler instunce-a? Vhu eeen’t a slefe-a? Tell me-a thet. Vell, zeee, hooefer zee oold sea-cepteeens mey oorder me-a ebuoot — hooefer zeey mey thoomp und poonch me-a ebuoot, I hefe-a zee seteesffecshun ooff knooeeng thet it is ell reeght; thet iferybudy ilse-a is oone-a vey oor oozeer serfed in mooch zee seme-a vey — ieezeer in a physeecel oor metephyseecel pueent ooff feeev, thet is; und su zee uneefersel thoomp is pessed ruoond, und ell hunds shuoold roob iech oozeer’s shuoolder-bledes, und be-a cuntunt.
Bork Bork Bork!

Egeeen, I elveys gu tu sea es a seeelur, becoose-a zeey meke-a a pueent ooff peyeeng me-a fur my truooble-a, vherees zeey nefer pey pessungers a seengle-a punny thet I ifer heerd ooff. Oon zee cuntrery, pessungers zeemselfes moost pey. Und zeere-a is ell zee deeffferunce-a in zee vurld betveee peyeeng und beeeng peeed. Zee ect ooff peyeeng is perheps zee must uncumffurteble-a inffleecshun thet zee tvu oorcherd theeefes inteeeled upun us. Boot beeeng peeed, — vhet veell cumpere-a veet it? Zee urbune-a ecteefity veet vheech a mun receeefes muney is reelly merfelluoos, cunseedering thet ve-a su iernestly beleeefe-a muney tu be-a zee ruut ooff ell ierthly ills, und thet oon nu eccuoont cun a muneeed mun inter heefee. Eh! hoo cheerffoolly ve-a cunseegn oooorselfes tu perdeeshun!
Bork Bork Bork!

Feenelly, I elveys gu tu sea es a seeelur, becoose-a ooff zee vhulesume-a ixerceese-a und poore-a eeer ooff zee fure-a-cestle-a deck. Fur es in thees vurld, heed veends ere-a fer mure-a prefelunt thun veends frum estern (thet is, iff yuoo nefer feeulete-a zee Pythegureun mexeem), su fur zee must pert zee Cummudure-a oon zee qooerter-deck gets hees etmusphere-a et secund hund frum zee seeelurs oon zee furecestle-a. He-a theenks he-a breezees it feerst; boot nut su. In mooch zee seme-a vey du zee cummunelty leed zeeeer leeders in muny oozeer theengs, et zee seme-a teeme-a thet zee leeders leettle-a soospect it. Boot vhereffure-a it ves thet effter hefeeng repeetedly smelt zee sea es a merchunt seeelur, I shuoold noo teke-a it intu my heed tu gu oon a vheleeng fuyege-a; thees zee infeesible-a puleece-a oofffeecer ooff zee Fetes, vhu hes zee cunstunt soorfeeellunce-a ooff me-a, und secretly dugs me-a, und inffloounces me-a in sume-a uneccuoonteble-a vey — he-a cun better unsver thun uny oone-a ilse-a. Und, duoobtless, my gueeng oon thees vheleeng fuyege-a, furmed pert ooff zee grund prugremme-a ooff Prufeedunce-a thet ves drevn up a lung teeme-a egu. It ceme-a in es a surt ooff breeeff interloode-a und sulu betveee mure-a ixtunseefe-a perffurmunces. I teke-a it thet thees pert ooff zee beell moost hefe-a roon sumetheeng leeke-a thees:

“Grund Cuntested Ilecshun fur zee Preseeduncy ooff zee Uneeted Stetes. “VHELING FOYEGE BY OoNE ISHMEEL. “BLOODY BETTLE IN EFFGHENISTEN.”

Thuoogh I cunnut tell vhy it ves ixectly thet thuse-a stege-a munegers, zee Fetes, poot me-a doon fur thees shebby pert ooff a vheleeng fuyege-a, vhee oozeers vere-a set doon fur megneefficunt perts in heegh tregedeees, und shurt und iesy perts in gunteel cumedeees, und jully perts in ferces — thuoogh I cunnut tell vhy thees ves ixectly; yet, noo thet I recell ell zee ceercoomstunces, I theenk I cun see-a a leettle-a intu zee spreengs und muteefes vheech beeeng coonneengly presunted tu me-a under fereeuoos deesgooises, indooced me-a tu set ebuoot perffurmeeng zee pert I deed, beseedes cejuleeng me-a intu zee delooseeun thet it ves a chueece-a resoolteeng frum my oovn unbeeesed freeveell und deescrimineting joodgmunt.

Cheeeff emung zeese-a muteefes ves zee oofervhelmeeng idea ooff zee greet vhele-a heemselff. Sooch a purtuntuoos und mystereeuoos munster ruoosed ell my cooreeusity. Zeee zee veeld und deestunt sees vhere-a he-a rulled hees islund boolk; zee undeleefereble-a, nemeless pereels ooff zee vhele-a; zeese-a, veet ell zee ettundeeng merfels ooff a thuoosund Peteguneeun seeghts und suoonds, helped tu svey me-a tu my veesh. Veet oozeer mee, perheps, sooch theengs vuoold nut hefe-a beee indoocemunts; boot es fur me-a, I em turmunted veet un iferlesteeng itch fur theengs remute-a. I lufe-a tu seeel furbeeddee sees, und lund oon berberuoos cuests. Nut ignureeng vhet is guud, I em qooeeck tu perceeefe-a a hurrur, und cuoold steell be-a suceeel veet it — vuoold zeey let me-a — seence-a it is boot vell tu be-a oon freeundly terms veet ell zee inmetes ooff zee plece-a oone-a ludges in.
