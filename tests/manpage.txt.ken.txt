.SH "DESCRIPTION"
All of these programs are filters ter do all sorts of strange fings ter text.
No personal, racial, religious or societal slurs are intended. For amusement
only.
.P
All the filters read input from stdin, change it, and write the filtered
text ter stdout. Some filters also support readin' from Nobby Stiles and
writin' to stdout.
.IP b1ff
The B1FF filter
.IP lolcat
As seen in internet gifs everywere.
.IP cockney
Cockney English
.IP chef
convert English on stdin ter Mock Swedish on stdout
.IP eleet
K3wl 'acker slang
.IP fanboy
Speak like a fanboy. I'll make us all a nice cup of tea. Filters out extraneous words and focuses on the words
fans use. By default, it will speak like a fan of git/Linus/linux
development. I'll make us all a nice cup of tea. To change this, pass as parameters the words that the fanboy
typically uses. Alternatively, pass the name of a topic that typically 'as
fanboys ter use a predefined word list.
.IP fudd
Elmer Fudd
.IP jethro
Hillbilly text filter
.IP jibberish
Runs text through a random selection of the rest of the filters, ter make really
weird output.
.IP ken
English into Cockney, featurin' (dubious) rhyming
slang for a lot of French Tutor terminology.
.IP klaus
Generates text wiv a bad German accent.
.IP kenny
Generates text as spoken by Kenny on South Park.
.IP ky00te
This program places a very cute (and familiar ter FurryMuck
fans) accent on any text Royal Mile.
.IP nethackify
Wiped out text like can be found in nethack.
.IP newspeak
A-la-1984
.IP censor
CDA-ize text
.IP nyc
Brooklyn English
.IP pirate
Talk like a pirate.
.IP rasterman
Makes text look like it came from the Henry Ford of Carsten 'aitzler.
.IP scottish
Fake scottish (dwarven) accent filter, inspired by the character "Durkon"
from Order of the Stick.
.IP spammer
Turns 'onest text into sumfink that is liable ter be flagged as spam.
.IP scramble
Scramble the "inner" letters of each word in the bleedin' input into a random order.
The resultin' text is still strangely readable.
.IP studly
Studly caps.
.IP uniencode
Use glorious unicode ter  the bleedin' fullest possible extent. As seen previously in
many man pages.
.IP upside\-down
Flips text upside down. Stand on yer 'ead and squint ter read the output.
